Meal Planner

Plan your meals for the week and stay on track with your nutrition goals. Our planner helps you organize breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks efficiently.

How to Use the Planner:

  • Choose meals for each day of the week.
  • Adjust portions according to your dietary needs.
  • Automatically generate a shopping list.
  • Track calories, macros, and nutrients.
