Tips for Healthy Eating

Follow these tips to maintain a balanced diet effortlessly:

  1. Include a variety of fruits and vegetables daily.
  2. Choose whole grains over refined carbs.
  3. Incorporate lean proteins like chicken, fish, tofu, and legumes.
  4. Limit sugar and processed foods.
  5. Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
